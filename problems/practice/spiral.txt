PROBLEM:
Given an n-by-m matrix, gather all elements in the matrix such that each element
is traversed in a spiral.

For the following example:
1 2 3, 4 5 6, 7 8 9

represents this matrix:
  [
    [ 1, 2, 3 ],
    [ 4, 5, 6 ],
    [ 7, 8, 9 ]
  ]

The solution to this is: 1 2 3 6 9 8 7 4 5
